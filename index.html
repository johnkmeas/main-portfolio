About Me

    I've been working in the t-shirt printing industry for too long and just got tired of the monotony. I decided to look for a new career, one that would make use of all I had to offer. I've had a mild obsession with digital music technology as a hobby and thought maybe I could find something comparable in an abstract way.

    Then one day, I noticed there were similarities with music and coding. As I explored this hypothesis, I came to the realization that I could possibly learn to program and become a web developer.

    Now I'd rather debug code than clean ink!

    Despite the onset of self-doubt and fatigue from full-time manual labour, I was able to complete a certificate specializing in Front-end Web Development through Udacity online training. I'm currently focused on Wordpress and I regularly research anything related to web development such as design, SEO, content marketing, copy writing and project management.
